My Expertise

A brief overview of the areas I work with most confidently and consistently.

CorelDRAW – My Main Strength

I use CorelDRAW as my primary tool in most design projects. I am confident with layout, vector work, print preparation, color optimization, and preparing files for different printed products.

Design & Image Handling

I use Photoshop and Illustrator to edit, refine, and finalize graphic elements. They support my main work in CorelDRAW and help me handle the details needed throughout the design process.

File Export & Output Preparation

I can export files in various formats for print, digital use, advertising, and social media. I focus on clean files, correct specifications, and formats that are easy for others to work with.
